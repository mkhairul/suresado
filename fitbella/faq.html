FITBella Tips

  1. Drinking FITBella AM before meal to keep you hydrated and helps you feel full more quickly, which may prevent you from overeating. Studies also show that people who maintain their hydration demonstrate better cognitive and physical performance.

    Drinking water can boost your metabolism by 24-30% over a period of 1-1.5 hours, burning off more calories. Drink a half liter (17oz) of water about a half an hour before meals may help you eat fewer calories and lose up to 44% more weight.

  2. For better result try swapping soda or concentrated juice for a glass of plain water instead. Drinking plain water in place of sugary drinks may help you tremendously with your weight management.

  3. Forget About Working Out if it scares the hell out of you! Instead Burn calories and invigorate muscles by:

  • Doing house work.
  • Climb stairs instead of taking the lift.
  • Go window shopping.
  • Beachcombing, riding bikes, hiking, dancing.
  • Gardening.
  • Washing the car.
  • Hike the mall, make sure to hit all the levels.
  • Take the stairs every chance you get.
  • Park your car at the back of the lot.
  • Sign up for charity walks
  • Get off the bus a few stops earlier.

It all adds up. If you walk twice a day for 10 minutes and try a few of these tips, you may find yourself with a low-impact, 30 minutes workout easily tucked under your belt.
